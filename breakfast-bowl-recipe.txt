Ingredients

	55g Oats
	1 scoop vegan whey protein
	30g hemp seeds
	2 cups boiling water
	50g granola
	75g frozen blueberries
	75g frozen raspberries

How to Prepare

	1. Put the oats, whey, and hemp seeds into a bowl and add the boiling water afterwards. Stir and let it rest for 1 minute.
	2. Add the granola, then the frozen fruits.
	3. Enjoy your delicious vegan breakfast.
